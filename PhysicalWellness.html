The greatest wealth is health

As the World Health Organization defines it, “Wellness is a state of complete physical, mental, and social well-being, and not merely the absence of disease or infirmity.” To achieve wellness, one has to continuously be aware and dedicated to making choices for a healthy and fulfilling life. A good starting point is to set goals and make plans to achieve them.

A healthy lifestyle should include an exercise routine, a balanced nutrition, routine health checkup and Stress management.

Choose you!

Fitness is necessary for good health. Exercising can help you feel better mentally, protect you from heart disease, stroke, obesity, diabetes, and high blood pressure. It can make you look younger, increase and maintain your bone density, improve your quality of life, and keep you from getting sick.

Get fit, Be active!

Schedule 30 minutes of moderate activity at least three times a week.

  • It helps maintain healthy muscles, joints and bone.
  • it improves mood and energy levels and helps reduce stress.
  • It decreases risks of heart disease,diabetes and high blood pressure.
You are what you eat!

Good nutrition has a big effect on your health and quality of life.

  • Balance your diet with fruits, vegetables, whole grains, lean proteins, legumes and nuts
  • Limit the consumption of empty calorie foods (Junk food and processed food).
Illness Prevention

Be a partner in your health plan with your healthcare provider.

  • Go to recommended check ups (physical, eye and dental).
  • Limit use of alcohol and tobacco products.
  • Listen to your body and monitor changes. Respond appropriately to signs of illness.
