Nutrition

Nutrition plays an important part in our overall journey to healthy living. You are either building your body up or tearing it down with what you consume through your mouth. You are what you eat, and studies have suggested that those who consume high portions of fast food are more likely to be depressed that those who eat fresh produce. Processed sugars and refined carbs spike the risk for someone to have depression, if they are a major part of their diet. To combat a poor diet, try eating foods kissed by the sun, fruits and vegetables. The radiance they receive, you will feel.

Good Sleep

Sleep, a time of rest, for the both the body and mind, plays a pivotal point to your overall health. While your body is engaged in a period of rest, it rejuvenates. Many mothers around the world can testify that a full night’s sleep does wonders for their child, as well as themselves. Just like mommy, sleep, whether at night, a during nap time improves a child’s overall mood. Three key elements of good sleep are • Boost immune system • Improves productivity • Reduces stress.
